Offline frequency file en_subtlex_zipf.json is derived from SUBTLEX-US word-form norms
(subtitle corpus; log10 frequency column Lg10WF).

Citation: Brysbaert, M., & New, B. (2009). Moving beyond Kučera and Francis: A critical
evaluation of current word frequency norms and the introduction of a new and improved word
frequency measure for American English. Behavior Research Methods, 41(4), 977–990.

The openlexicon project documents CC-BY-SA for this dataset; verify terms before redistribution.
Build: npm run build:subtlex
